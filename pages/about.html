Our Story

Welcome to 124 By Moonine Homes, a boutique homestay nestled in the heart of historic Taiping, Perak. Our journey began with a simple vision: to create a space where guests can experience the warmth of Malaysian hospitality while enjoying modern amenities and timeless design.

Taiping, known as the "City of Everlasting Peace," has a rich history as Malaysia's first hill station and the wettest town in the country. Our homestay is perfectly positioned to let you explore this heritage city's many treasures - from the iconic Taiping Lake Gardens to the renowned Zoo Taiping & Night Safari.

Every corner of our home has been thoughtfully designed with our guests in mind, from the serene mid-century modern aesthetic to the carefully curated amenities that make your stay memorable.
