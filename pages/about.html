Our Story

Welcome to 124 By Moonine Homes, a spacious 4900 sqft single-storey Semi-D located in Kamunting, Taiping, Perak. Hosted by Kelvin, a Superhost with 8 years of experience and a perfect 5.0 rating from 42 reviews, our home is designed to accommodate up to 14 guests with 4 bedrooms, 6 beds, and 3 bathrooms.

Perfectly positioned in a prime location - just 2 minutes from AEON, Lotus, and Taiping Sentral Mall, 5 minutes from the KTM station, and 7 minutes from the famous Zoo Taiping & Night Safari and Lake Gardens. Whether you're planning a family reunion, birthday party, corporate retreat, or a cozy weekend getaway, our home is the ideal venue.

Ranked in the top 10% of homes on Airbnb as a Guest Favorite, every corner of our home has been thoughtfully designed with our guests in mind - from the entertainment facilities to the fully equipped kitchen and comfortable sleeping arrangements.
