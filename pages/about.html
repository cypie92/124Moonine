Our Story

Welcome to 124 By Moonine Homes, a beautifully renovated 4900 sqft single-storey Semi-D located in Taman Taiping, Perak. Our home is strategically located with restaurants, cafes, shopping malls, and pubs just around the corner, making it the perfect base for your Taiping adventure.

Designed to accommodate up to 14 guests with 3 bedrooms + 1 attic bedroom, 6 beds, and 3 bathrooms (1 private ensuite + 2 shared), our home offers the perfect blend of space and comfort. Whether you're planning a family reunion, birthday party, corporate retreat, or a cozy weekend getaway, our home is the ideal venue.

Ranked in the top 10% of homes on Airbnb as a Guest Favorite, every corner of our home has been thoughtfully designed with our guests in mind - from the entertainment facilities to the fully equipped kitchen and comfortable sleeping arrangements.
